Gobi Manchurian

Description

Gobi Manchurian is a popular Indo Chinese appetizer made with cauliflower corn flour,soy sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce, ginger and garlic.To make this recipe, cauliflower is first coated in batter & then deep fried until crisp.Then these are tossed in sweet, sour and hot manchurian sauce.

Though a manchurian recipe can be made using chicken, mixed vegetable, soya chunks, fish etc-this one made with cauliflower has very much been loved and raved by most Indians. The aroma and taste of fried cauliflower makes gobi manchurian a unique & super delicious appetizer.
