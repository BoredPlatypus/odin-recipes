Gobi Manchurian

Description

Gobi Manchurian is a popular Indo Chinese appetizer made with cauliflower corn flour,soy sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce, ginger and garlic.To make this recipe, cauliflower is first coated in batter & then deep fried until crisp.Then these are tossed in sweet, sour and hot manchurian sauce.

Though a manchurian recipe can be made using chicken, mixed vegetable, soya chunks, fish etc-this one made with cauliflower has very much been loved and raved by most Indians. The aroma and taste of fried cauliflower makes gobi manchurian a unique & super delicious appetizer.

Ingredients

A. Fried Cauliflower

  • oil for deep frying
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 15 cauliflower florets

B. Manchurian Sauce

  • 3/4 cup vegetable stock, divided
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 green chile pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger
  • 1 green onion, chopped, or to taste (Optional)
